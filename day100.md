## ☀️ Morning Block (2 hours): Follow Up Everything

### Follow-Up Priority

1. **Applications 7+ days old** → Send polite follow-up on LinkedIn or email
2. **Applications with recruiter contact** → Direct message
3. **Referral applications** → Ask your referrer to check status

### Follow-Up Message

```
Hi [Recruiter],

I applied for the Data Engineer role at [Company] on [date]. 
I'm following up to check if there are any updates on my application.

I'm still very interested in the position and have been continuing 
to build my skills. My portfolio is at github.com/yourname.

Thank you for your time!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

---

## 🌤️ Afternoon Block (2 hours): Interview Prep (If Scheduled)

### If You Have an Interview Coming Up

**The Night Before:**
1. Review the company research (Day 95 notes)
2. Do 5 SQL warm-up problems (easy ones for confidence)
3. Review your project pitches
4. Prepare 3-5 questions to ask them
5. Get enough sleep!

**Interview Day Tips:**
- Arrive 10 minutes early (virtual: test setup 15 min before)
- Bring a notepad and pen
- Have your GitHub portfolio open in a browser tab
- Speak slowly and clearly
- It's OK to say "let me think about that for a moment"
- Always ask clarifying questions before coding

### If No Interviews Yet

Keep applying! Don't get discouraged. The average time from application to first interview is 2-3 weeks. You started applying only 4 days ago.

Apply to 5-10 more positions.
