## ☀️ Morning Block (2 hours): Cover Letter Template

### The Formula

```
Paragraph 1 (2-3 sentences): Why THIS company? (show you researched them)
Paragraph 2 (4-5 sentences): Why YOU? (match your skills to their requirements)  
Paragraph 3 (2-3 sentences): Call to action + availability
```

### Master Template

```
Dear [Hiring Manager's Name / Hiring Team],

I'm writing to express my interest in the [Job Title] position at [Company]. 
[1 sentence about why this company interests you — be specific].

[Company] is doing exciting work in [specific area from their website/news], 
and I'm particularly drawn to [specific project, product, or mission]. As 
someone who [personal connection to their domain — e.g., "uses GrabFood daily" 
or "follows Southeast Asia's data scene"], I'd love to contribute to your 
data team.

I bring hands-on experience building end-to-end data solutions:
- Built 5 portfolio projects using SQL, Python, AWS, dbt, and Airflow
- Designed a star schema data warehouse with SCD Type 2 for historical tracking
- Created a serverless AWS pipeline (S3 → Glue → Athena) processing 50K+ records
- Set up CI/CD with GitHub Actions for automated pipeline testing
- Implemented data quality checks using Great Expectations and dbt tests

While I'm early in my career, I've invested [X months] in an intensive 
self-directed data engineering program covering the full modern data stack. 
My projects demonstrate that I can build production-quality pipelines — not 
just follow tutorials.

I'd welcome the opportunity to discuss how my skills can contribute to 
[Company]'s data engineering needs. I'm available for an interview at your 
convenience and can start [date / "immediately" / "from [date]"].

Thank you for considering my application.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Phone] | [Email] | [LinkedIn] | [GitHub]
```

## 🌤️ Afternoon Block (2 hours): Company Research Template

### 10-Minute Research Checklist

Before writing each cover letter, research the company:

1. **Company website** (2 min): What do they do? Mission? Values?
2. **Job description** (3 min): What are the top 3 required skills? What's the team like?
3. **LinkedIn company page** (2 min): Recent posts, size, growth
4. **News/articles** (2 min): Any recent funding, launches, or press?
5. **Tech blog** (1 min): If they have one, what tech do they write about?

### Personalization Ideas by Company Type

| Company Type | Personalization |
|-------------|----------------|
| Food delivery (GrabFood, Foodpanda) | "As a daily GrabFood user, I've always wondered how the recommendation system works..." |
| E-commerce (Shopee, Lazada) | "Shopee's growth in Southeast Asia is impressive — I'd love to work on the data infrastructure behind it" |
| Banking (DBS, OCBC, Maybank) | "DBS has been named 'World's Best Digital Bank' — I want to be part of that data-driven culture" |
| Consulting (FDM, Accenture) | "FDM's training program is well-known — the opportunity to work across different clients excites me" |
| Startup | "I'm drawn to [Company]'s mission to [X] — early-stage companies are where I can make the biggest impact" |
