## ☀️ Morning Block (2 hours): Interview or Practice

### If Interviewing Today

**Before the interview (30 min):**
- Review company notes
- Do 3 easy SQL problems for confidence
- Review your STAR stories
- Test your tech setup (if virtual)
- Take deep breaths

**During the interview:**
- Listen carefully to each question
- Ask clarifying questions before answering
- Think out loud (they want to see your process)
- If stuck, say "Let me approach this differently"
- Be honest about what you don't know
- Ask your prepared questions at the end

**After the interview:**
- Send thank-you email within 2 hours
- Note down questions you struggled with
- Update your tracker

### If Not Interviewing

**SQL practice (30 min):** 10 problems from LeetCode SQL or HackerRank

**System design review (30 min):** Re-read your cheat sheets from Day 89

**Apply to 5+ new positions**

### Weekly Review

How many applications sent this week? How many interviews? What's the response rate?

**Typical response rates:**
- Cold application: 10-15% get a response
- Referral application: 40-60% get a response
- Direct outreach: 20-30% get a response

Don't panic if response rate is low. This is normal.
